Directions

The Conference will be held at the Medical College of Wisconsin Main Campus. Guests can park in the MCW lot across from the main entrance (Google Maps Location for Parking). Enter the lot where the heart is located on the map below. The surface lot fills up quickly, but there should be plenty of space in the parking structure.

When you get to the gate, please press the intercom at the parking gate and let the person on the other end know they're here for Pain Research Day, and they will let you in. After Parking, make your way to the front MCW entrance (red star)(Google Maps Location for MCW Front Entrance). You will then need to use the intercom or tell security that you are here for Pain Research Day to gain access to the building. Once inside, you will see signs for Wisconsin Pain Research Day. Follow the signs to the registration table to check in and get your name badge.
